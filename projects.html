Crypto Bot

I had the opportunity to participate in a team project during a 24 hour hackathon: McHacks 2023, where we embarked in Tower Research Capital's challenge to make a chat bot. Our crypto chat bot is designed to provide cryptocurrency statistics, all through natural language interaction. In this endeavor, I had the opportunity to learn and take charge of the front-end development, using HTML and CSS to craft a simple and intuitive interface. Meanwhile, on the backend, I used Flask and OpenAI to bring life to the chat bot.

Reddit Bot

Introducing my first coding project! In the Winter 2022 semester at McGill, I had the chance to dive into the Reddit API and create a Reddit bot using Python. Inspired by the game Mad Libs, I programmed the bot to search for comments and replies it hasn't responded to within a particular thread. The bot then generates fun, random messages by filling in the blanks of multiple provided sentences using a provided bank of words. Additionally, the bot runs continuously as a daemon.
